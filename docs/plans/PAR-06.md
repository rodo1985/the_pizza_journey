# PAR-06 Polish And QA

## Purpose

Improve the feel, clarity, readability, and child-friendly usability of the game once the first playable systems are in place.

## Scope

- Tune difficulty and player feedback.
- Improve readability of menus, HUD, and quiz feedback.
- Add or refine animation hooks, sound hooks, and simple juice effects.
- Capture family playtest findings and turn them into actionable fixes.

## Dependencies

- `FND-05` must be complete because the first full gameplay and UI loop needs to exist before polish work is meaningful.

## Inputs And Interfaces Touched

- HUD feedback
- audio and animation hooks
- playtest checklist
- balance values and readability details

## Deliverables

- Polish backlog
- QA checklist
- Child-playability improvements
- Tuning fixes and feedback refinements

## Acceptance Checklist

- Core screens are easy for children to understand.
- Feedback for damage, quiz results, and level completion is clear.
- Playtest findings are documented and prioritized.
- The workstream improves feel without destabilizing the core systems.

## Out Of Scope

- Major architectural rewrites
- New headline gameplay systems
- Back-end services
- Large-scale art replacement

## Suggested Branch Name

`codex/polish-qa`
